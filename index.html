Lover of design, process, and coffee.

Elijah loves to create emotional and story-driven experiences. Much of their inspiration comes from nature and behavioral psychology which results in their love for incentive based designs.

Based in Tio’tia:ke since 2012, Elijah collaborates with agencies, small businesses and collectives to create high-quality products & experiences.

Elijah strives to learn from their environment and aims to share the knowledge they acquire. They believe that sharing is the seed of community.

That's why they take on barter & pro-bono projects!
